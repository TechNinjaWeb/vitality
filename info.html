Description

This application was created to help facilitate an individuals choice to live a healthier life.

Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being, not merely the absence of illness or disease. A healthy lifestyle is determined by 3 primary factors of health. Fitness, Nutrition, and Self-Image.

Fitness

Active living is a way of life that integrates physical activity into your everyday routines, such as walking to the store or biking to work. It is a combination of physical activity and recreation activities aimed at the general public to encourage a healthier lifestyle.

Nutrition

Proper nutrition can help your body maintain a healthy balance. It gives us the building blocks our body needs to grow and be strong. Most importantly, it plays an important role in preventing many diseases and health conditions.

Self-Image

Our self-esteem determines how valuable, loveable, worthwhile, and competent we feel we are. When we have healthy self-esteem, they feel more positive about their bodies and find it easier to make good decisions for themselves that promote health, including taking care of the needs of their bodies.
